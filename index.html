Our Basque Coast Getaway

Beaches, sunset walks, pintxos, quiet mountain views, and a road trip across the green north of Spain — made for slow mornings and beautiful evenings together.

See the map Open itinerary

The idea

A relaxed 8-day loop: fly from Prague to Bilbao, start with food and art, spend the most romantic nights in San Sebastián, add a dramatic Basque coast stop, enjoy one mountain-hiking day in Picos de Europa, and finish with beaches in Santander before returning to Bilbao.

Best moodRomantic coastal walks, beaches, dinners, and scenic drives.
Trip length8 days / 7 nights, easily expandable to 9 days.
TransportFlight to Bilbao + rental car after San Sebastián.
PaceSlow enough to relax, with only 1–2 hiking/sightseeing days.

Fly from Prague to Bilbao, settle in, and keep the first evening easy. Walk along the river, see the Guggenheim from outside at golden hour, then have a gentle pintxos dinner in Casco Viejo.

Days 2–4 · [Add dates]

San Sebastián: beaches, sunsets, and food

La ConchaOld TownRomantic base

Train or drive to San Sebastián and stay for three nights. This is the heart of the trip: slow beach mornings, La Concha promenade, Monte Igueldo views, and pintxos evenings in the Old Town.

  • Sunset walk from La Concha to Peine del Viento.
  • One relaxed beach afternoon at La Concha or Ondarreta.
  • Optional half-day to Hondarribia for colorful streets and a waterfront lunch.
Day 5 · [Add date]

Gaztelugatxe + Urdaibai coast

CliffsSea viewsPhoto stop

Leave San Sebastián and follow the coast west. Stop at San Juan de Gaztelugatxe for the dramatic island walk, then continue through the Urdaibai area — Mundaka and the coast around Bermeo are perfect for a beautiful, not-too-rushed day.

Day 6 · [Add date]

Picos de Europa: one mountain day

HikingMountain viewsNature

Drive into the mountains and stay around Cangas de Onís. Keep the hiking scenic rather than exhausting: Covadonga Lakes or a shorter viewpoint walk is ideal for one memorable mountain day without losing the relaxed vacation feeling.

Santander: beach finale

RelaxSeafoodBeach walks

Finish with two nights by the sea in Santander. Walk around the Magdalena Peninsula, relax on Sardinero Beach, and make the final evening all about a long dinner and a slow walk by the water.
